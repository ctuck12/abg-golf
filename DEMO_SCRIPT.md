# Full Version (~90 seconds)

---

## [Landing Screen]
> *Open the app on a mobile device*

"When you open the app, you land on the group selection screen. If you're on a phone, you'll see a quick guide walking you through how to add the app to your Home Screen — so it feels like a native app with no browser bar."

---

## [Group Login]
> *Tap a group, enter the password*

"Tap your group, enter the group password, and you're in. Every group has its own private space."

---

## [Leaderboard — Main View]
> *Show the live leaderboard*

"The leaderboard updates in real time as scores come in. You can see every player's score relative to par, with overall standings for the round front and center."

---

## [Side Game Chips — Skins / Banker / Daytona / Hammer]
> *Tap each chip to show the live view*

"Tap any of the side game chips — Skins, Banker, Daytona, or Hammer — to see live standings and who owes what. Everything rolls up into a single **Combined Settlements** view so there's no manual math at the end of the round."

---

## [Payouts Modal]
> *Tap the dollar amount in Combined Settlements*

"Tap the settlement total and you get a full breakdown — who pays who, down to the dollar. Tap any player's row to see their itemized breakdown by game."

---

## [Admin Hub]
> *Log in as admin, show the Admin Hub*

"Admins log in with the group admin password. From the Admin Hub you can: create and manage rounds, set up courses with hole-by-hole pars and handicap indices, and configure any side games for the round — Banker, Daytona, Hammer, Skins, and head-to-head matchups."

---

## [Score Entry]
> *Open the scorecard for an active round*

"During the round, the scorekeeper enters scores hole by hole. For games like Hammer, they see a live running tab in the header showing the current stake and who's up. They can throw the hammer, accept, or fold — all tracked automatically, with birdie and eagle multipliers applied in real time."

---

## [H2H Scorecards]
> *Tap the Scorecards tab, open a matchup*

"From the Scorecards tab, you can pull up any head-to-head matchup. The header shows both players and their handicaps side by side, and the scorecard tracks net scores hole by hole so everyone can follow the match."

---

## [Wrap]

"Everything's mobile-first, works as an installed app, and keeps everyone honest — no spreadsheets, no arguments, just golf."
